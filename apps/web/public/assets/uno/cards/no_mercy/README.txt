Separated custom UNO-style cards

- Each PNG is cropped from the uploaded sheets, background made transparent, and upscaled 2x.
- Files are grouped into folders by color: yellow, blue, green, red, wild.
- Duplicate cards are kept and numbered with _01, _02, etc.
- Some custom cards are labeled by best guess from the icons:
  swap = two-arrow exchange-style card
  discard_all = fan/stack of cards card
  wild_skip_all = black wild card with smiling colored cards (best-guess label)
  wild_reverse_plus4 = black +4 card with reverse arrows (best-guess label)
  wild_plus6 = black +6 card with six colored cards
